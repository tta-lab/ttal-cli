## Errors from Kiki round (do not repeat the pattern)
1. Newspaper double-use (wrapping at bakery vs unused on man's lap at seawall)
2. Blue gingham recognition (old lady "recognized" Osono's borrowed cloth)

## Discipline this round
- **Before each turn**: scan the World State Register and Prior Commits log. Pick the next beat in a way that does not contradict either.
- **After each turn**: append every NEW committed fact to Prior Commits. Things to watch:
  - Objects (what they are, who has them, where they are)
  - Time (when, weather, day part)
  - Geography (who is where)
  - Wardrobe / appearance
  - Statements anyone made (because future characters can reference them)
  - Numerical claims (ages, durations, counts)
- **If contradiction arises**: out-of-frame correction (🐱 stops the scene, names the conflict, fixes). NO in-world retcon to paper over.
